Swami Vivekanand College of Higher Education

Swami Vivekanand College of Higher Education established in 2014 and affiliated to “Chhatrapati Shahu Ji Maharaj University, Kanpur”. Swami Vivekanand College of Higher Education is the inspirational place to study where students are encouraged and supported to reach their highest possible potential. We maintain a high standard of education and created an environment for learning. Learning is the process of reshaping the attitude of students and giving them the opportunity to explore & rediscover them.
